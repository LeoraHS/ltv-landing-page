Instant Moving Estimates with AR/VR Technology

Packit.ai uses cutting-edge AR/VR to inventory your space, allowing you to get accurate, instant estimates for your move. Whether you're moving locally or across the country, we make it easy to plan every step of your move.

Get real-time estimates for:

  • Time: Know exactly how long your move will take.
  • Cost: Understand the full cost of your move upfront.
  • Truck Space: See how much space your items will take in a truck, helping you decide if you'd like to move yourself or hire professionals.
